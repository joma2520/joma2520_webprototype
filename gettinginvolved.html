    Save A Life - Donate Now

    Every year, millions of unwanted farmed animals suffer an unimaginable fate. These are the forgotten ones. No-one hears their cries. Most are deemed a waste product of the dairy industry, others are used as egg-laying machines their entire short life, never seeing natural daylight or feeling the sunshine on their feathers. You can help save and re-home unwanted farmed animals. Their lives are in your hands.
